About Us

Your trusted frozen food distributor and restaurant in Naga City

Our Story

Batangas Premium Naga - Noona's Food Station started with a simple mission: to provide the people of Naga City with premium quality frozen goods and delicious Filipino meals.

We understand the importance of quality food for families, which is why we source only the finest frozen meats, vegetables, and ingredients. Combined with our passion for authentic Filipino cuisine, we've become a trusted name in Naga City, Camarines Sur.

Whether you're looking for frozen goods to stock your kitchen or craving a delicious home-cooked Filipino meal, Batangas Premium Naga has you covered.
