Welcome to Batangas Premium Naga, your premier destination for high-quality frozen goods and delicious ready-to-eat meals in Naga City, Camarines Sur.

Established with a commitment to providing the community with premium frozen food products and authentic Filipino meals, we take pride in our careful selection of products and exceptional customer service.

Whether you're looking for frozen meats, vegetables, or craving our signature Filipino meals, we’ve got you covered with quality products at competitive prices.

Our Mission

To provide the people of Naga City with access to premium quality frozen goods and delicious, authentic Filipino meals while ensuring excellent customer service and reliable delivery.
